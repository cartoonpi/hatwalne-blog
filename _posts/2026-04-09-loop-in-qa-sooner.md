When working on cross-functional or cross-team projects, loop in the QA from the dependent team or the other team sooner. 
* Often, they know the product and the edge cases that might arise from the new development better. They can help. 
* They are typically more helpful than the brat software engineers from the dependent team whose OKRs are not moved by collaborating with you.
* And in the end, the changes that you make need to go through their regression, so if they are not in the loop earlier, they can block your release anyways. This is a note to self.
