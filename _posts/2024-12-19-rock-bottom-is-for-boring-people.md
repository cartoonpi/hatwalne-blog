* I would not read this post when I am down.*

So, you think you are at rock bottom? You think things cannot get worse than this? well I wish that they don't but there are still tonnes of ways your life can get worse. If you think it can't. you just lack creativity. Ya plain borin

Lost your job suddenly when you have a kid on the way? think this is the worst it can get? Just wait to trip over an uneven sidewalk and break your ankle.

The love of your life cheated on you? Think your life is over? The guy she cheated with could have had aids. You just drilled through the rock bottom and are bathing in hot lava.

Things can always get worse, and things can always get better. You really have no control. Any expectations you have from future do not come with manufacturer's warranty. Indulge in hopium or pessimisium at your own risk.
