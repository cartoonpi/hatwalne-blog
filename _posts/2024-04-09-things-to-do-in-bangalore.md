Making this page for myself to refer to when I have free time on a weekend and I am in mood to socialize. Below is a list of venues and events that are usually 'happening' or ways to find such events. I am not associated with any and do not guarantee anything. Will keep updating this list.

  1. Cubbon Reads: The OG reads initiative. Sundays Morning 9 till 2. Follow [their insta page](<https://www.instagram.com/cubbonreads/?hl=en>) for announcements
  2. Bangalore International Center in Indiranagar do host variety of lectures and events. Most of them are free to attend.[ Follow Instagram for updates.](<https://www.instagram.com/bicblr/>)
  3. Lahe Lahe in Indiranagar hosts variety of events. I have only been to their Open mics which are pretty good. 
  4. Jagriti Theater
  5. [Atta Galatta ](<https://www.instagram.com/attagalatta/?hl=en>)book store will host variety of events and authors
  6. [Small World. ](<https://www.instagram.com/small.world/?hl=en>)Open mics, Speed dating and more.
  7. [E Chai](<https://echai.ventures/cities/bangalore>) : Startup networking and gyan events
  8. [Listner's Circle](<https://www.meetup.com/listeners-circle-bangalore/>): Fairly open-ended events to meet new people
