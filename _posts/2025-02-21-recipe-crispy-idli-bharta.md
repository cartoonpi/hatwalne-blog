A quick and delicious way to repurpose leftover baingan bharta and idlis into a flavorful, crispy stir-fry.

#### **Ingredients:**

  * **Leftover baingan bharta** – about ⅓ portion of a large baingan bharta
  * **Day-old idlis** – 2-3, chopped into bite-sized pieces
  * **Oil** – 1-2 tbsp (for shallow frying)

#### **Instructions:**

  1. **Prep the Idlis** : Chop the day-old idlis into smaller, bite-sized pieces. They may feel dry and stiff, but that helps them crisp up well.
  2. **Shallow Fry the Idlis** : Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the idli pieces and cook, turning occasionally, until they develop a golden-brown crispy crust on all sides.
  3. **Add the Bharta** : Once the idlis are crisp, add the leftover baingan bharta to the pan. Stir well to coat the idli pieces in the smoky, spiced bharta.
  4. **Cook & Combine**: Let it cook for 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
  5. **Serve** : Enjoy hot as a snack or light meal.

This dish has the perfect balance of crispy idli texture and the rich, smoky flavors of baingan bharta.
